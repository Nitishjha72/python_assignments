12. Write a short program that prints the numbers 1 to 10 using a for loop. Then write an equivalent program that prints the numbers 1 to 10 using a while loop.
	Answer:
		For loop:
		
		for i in range(1,11):
			print(i)
			
		While loop:
		
		i=1
		while(i<=10):
			print(i)
			i+=1
